Welcome to BahaiStudy.Group,

a website designed to help you connect with individuals who want to study the Writings in a group setting.

Note that this website is not functional at this time but we are creating it now. Study groups will be held virtually, are open to everyone, and there is no cost or other requirements to join. The group can decide what specific format the meetings should have, click here for a suggested format.
